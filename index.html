💡 Tips for Mrs. Williams

• Answer Keys: Every worksheet, quiz, and test has a hidden answer key — click "Show/Hide Answer Key" at the bottom.

• iPad Distribution: Share this portal link with students. They can open worksheets and type answers directly.

• Quizzes on iPads: Students can complete quizzes in their browser. For tests, consider having them work on paper for show-your-work sections.

• Differentiation: Each lesson plan includes suggestions for struggling and advanced students.

• Real-World Contexts: Every worksheet uses practical scenarios — pizza math, budgets, baking, sports, and more!
