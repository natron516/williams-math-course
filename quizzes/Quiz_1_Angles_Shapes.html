Quiz 1: Angles & Shapes (10 questions)

1 An angle that measures 135° is:
A) Acute
B) Right
C) Obtuse
D) Straight
2 Two complementary angles add up to:
A) 90°
B) 180°
C) 270°
D) 360°
3 One angle is 72°. What is its supplement?
4 Two lines cross. One angle is 55°. What is the angle directly across from it?
5 A triangle with all sides equal is called:
A) Scalene
B) Isosceles
C) Equilateral
D) Right
6 A triangle has angles of 40° and 85°. What is the third angle?
7 A triangle with one 90° angle is classified by angles as:
A) Acute
B) Obtuse
C) Right
D) Equilateral
8 What is the interior angle sum of a pentagon (5 sides)?
9 Two angles are complementary. One is 3 times the other. Find both angles.
10 A regular hexagon has 6 equal angles. What is each angle?
Hint: angle sum = (n-2) × 180°
Score: ____ / 10

🔑 Answer Key

1. C) Obtuse | 2. A) 90° | 3. 180° − 72° = 108°

4. 55° (vertical angles) | 5. C) Equilateral | 6. 180° − 40° − 85° = 55°

7. C) Right | 8. (5−2) × 180° = 540°

9. x + 3x = 90, x = 22.5° and 67.5° | 10. 720° ÷ 6 = 120°
