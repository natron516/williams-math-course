📐 Scope & Sequence

8 Weeks · 3 Sessions/Week · 1 Hour Each · 24 Sessions Total

📐 UNIT 1: GEOMETRY — Weeks 1–4
Week 1: Angles & Shapes▶
1MonIntro to Geometry & Angles
• Identify types of angles (acute, right, obtuse, straight)
• Measure angles with a protractor
• Understand angle relationships
🍕 Pizza Slice Angles — estimate and measure angles of pizza slices, pie pieces, and clock hands
2WedAngle Relationships
• Complementary & supplementary angles
• Vertical angles
• Solve for unknown angles
🏠 Roof Pitch Problems — find missing angles in house roof designs and ramp constructions
3FriClassifying Triangles & Polygons
• Classify triangles by sides and angles
• Identify regular polygons
• Interior angle sums
⚽ Sports Geometry — find shapes in soccer fields, basketball courts, and game boards
📝 Quiz 1
Week 2: Perimeter & Area▶
4MonPerimeter of Polygons
• Calculate perimeter of rectangles, triangles, irregular shapes
• Convert units within problems
• Perimeter word problems
🌿 Garden Planning — design a garden bed with specific perimeter constraints and fencing budget
5WedArea: Rectangles & Triangles
• Area formulas for rectangles, squares, triangles
• Decompose complex shapes
• Area word problems
🎨 Room Makeover — calculate paint needed for walls, carpet for floors (real room dimensions)
6FriArea: Parallelograms & Trapezoids
• Derive area formulas from rectangles
• Apply to real measurements
• Composite shape areas
🧁 Baking Pans — figure out how much batter fills different pan shapes, compare pan sizes
📝 Quiz 2
Week 3: Circles & Composite Shapes▶
7MonCircles: Circumference
• Understand pi (π)
• Circumference formula (C = πd or 2πr)
• Real-world circumference problems
🚲 Bike Wheel Math — how far does a bike travel per wheel rotation? Compare tire sizes
8WedCircles: Area
• Area of a circle (A = πr²)
• Estimate and calculate
• Compare circular vs rectangular spaces
🍪 Cookie & Pizza Math — which is a better deal: one 16" pizza or two 10" pizzas?
9FriComposite Shapes
• Break down complex shapes into simple ones
• Add and subtract areas
• Real-world floor plans
🏗️ Blueprint Challenge — calculate total area of an L-shaped room or irregular yard
📝 Quiz 3
Week 4: Volume & Surface Area▶
10Mon3D Shapes & Volume of Prisms
• Identify 3D shapes (prisms, cylinders, pyramids)
• Volume of rectangular prisms (V = lwh)
• Volume word problems
📦 Shipping & Storage — which box fits more? Compare shipping box sizes for cost efficiency
11WedVolume of Cylinders & Surface Area Intro
• Volume of cylinders (V = πr²h)
• Surface area of rectangular prisms
• Nets of 3D shapes
🥤 Drink Container Design — which cup holds more: tall & thin or short & wide?
12FriGeometry Review & Test
• Review all geometry concepts
• Problem-solving with mixed geometry
• Test preparation
Mixed review problems covering all 4 weeks
🔴 TEST 1: Geometry
📊 UNIT 2: INTRO TO ALGEBRA — Weeks 5–8
Week 5: Variables & Expressions▶
13MonIntro to Variables & Expressions
• Understand variables as unknowns
• Write algebraic expressions from words
• Evaluate expressions
🎮 Game Score Algebra — write expressions for point systems (3 coins + 2 gems = ?)
14WedCombining Like Terms
• Identify like terms
• Simplify expressions
• Translate word problems to expressions
🛒 Shopping Cart Math — simplify a grocery list with pricing expressions
15FriOrder of Operations with Variables
• Apply PEMDAS with variables
• Evaluate multi-step expressions
• Write and simplify
🍳 Recipe Scaling — use expressions to double, triple, or halve recipes
📝 Quiz 4
Week 6: One-Step Equations▶
16MonSolving One-Step Equations (+/−)
• Addition and subtraction equations
• Inverse operations
• Check solutions
💰 Budget Balancing — find the missing expense when you know the total budget
17WedSolving One-Step Equations (×/÷)
• Multiplication and division equations
• Inverse operations
• Word problems
🍕 Fair Shares — split costs, divide materials evenly, find unit prices
18FriMixed One-Step Equations
• All four operation types mixed
• Writing equations from word problems
• Multi-context practice
🏪 Lemonade Stand — set prices, calculate profits, find break-even amounts
📝 Quiz 5
Week 7: Two-Step Equations & Inequalities▶
19MonTwo-Step Equations
• Solve equations with two operations
• Order of inverse operations
• Real-world two-step problems
📱 Phone Plan Math — base cost + per-minute rate: how many minutes can you afford?
20WedIntro to Inequalities
• Inequality symbols (<, >, ≤, ≥)
• Solve one-step inequalities
• Graph on a number line
🎢 Height Requirements — model "you must be at least this tall" as inequalities
21FriEquations & Inequalities Practice
• Mixed practice with equations and inequalities
• Word problem translation
• Error analysis
🎯 Equation Detective — find and fix mistakes in solved problems
📝 Quiz 6 🔴 TEST 2: Algebra Part 1
Week 8: Coordinate Plane & Review▶
22MonThe Coordinate Plane
• Understand x-axis, y-axis, origin
• Plot ordered pairs in all 4 quadrants
• Identify coordinates from graphs
🗺️ Treasure Map — plot coordinates to find hidden treasure locations on a grid
23WedIntro to Linear Relationships
• Create input/output tables
• Plot points from tables
• Recognize linear patterns
🚗 Road Trip Math — graph distance vs. time at different speeds, predict arrival times
📝 Quiz 7
24FriCourse Review & Final Test
• Review all algebra concepts
• Mixed geometry and algebra problems
• Final assessment
Comprehensive review covering full 8 weeks
