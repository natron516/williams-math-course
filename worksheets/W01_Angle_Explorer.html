Part 3: Clock Angles

⏰ Clock hands create angles!

There are 12 hours on a clock. A full circle = 360°, so each hour mark = 30° apart. Count the hour marks between the hands!

11What angle do the hands make at 3:00?
💡 From 12 to 3 = how many hour marks × 30°?
12What angle do the hands make at 6:00?
13What angle do the hands make at 1:00?
💡 1 hour mark × 30° = ?
🤯 Fun fact: A full rotation is 360°. Ancient Babylonians chose 360 because it has tons of factors (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12…) making it super easy to divide evenly!
