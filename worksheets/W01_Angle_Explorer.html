🍕 Part 2: Pizza Math

🍕 A whole pizza cut into 8 equal slices

A full circle = 360°. If you divide it equally into slices, each slice is the same angle. Think: 360° ÷ number of slices!

9 What is the angle of each pizza slice?
💡 360° ÷ 8 slices = ?
10 If you eat 3 slices, what angle does the remaining pizza form?
💡 5 remaining slices × angle per slice = ?
🕐 Part 3: Clock Angles

⏰ Clock hands create angles!

A clock face is a circle (360°). There are 12 hours, so each hour mark is 30° apart. The angle between the hands depends on how many hour marks apart they are.

11 What angle do the hands make at 3:00?
💡 How many hour marks from 12 to 3?
12 What angle do the hands make at 6:00?
13 What angle do the hands make at 1:00?
💡 1 hour mark × 30° = ?
🤯 Fun fact: A full rotation is 360°. Ancient Babylonians chose 360 because it has tons of factors (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12...) making it easy to divide evenly!
✂️ Part 4: Angles All Around You

✂️ Scissors create angles!

When scissors open, the blades form an angle at the pivot point. The wider they open, the bigger the angle.

14 If scissors are open to a 40° angle, is this acute, right, or obtuse?
15 If scissors are fully open at 170°, what type of angle is this?
