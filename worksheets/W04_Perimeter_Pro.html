🏃 A running track goes around a rectangular field.
11 The field is 100 m × 60 m. How far is one lap around the field?

13 Baseboard is being installed around a room that's 14 ft × 11 ft. There are two doorways (each 3 ft wide) with no baseboard. How many feet of baseboard are needed?
14 A triangular plot of land has sides of 150 ft, 200 ft, and 250 ft. The owner wants to put up a chain-link fence at $8.75 per foot. What's the total cost?
15 A rectangular swimming pool is 25 m × 12 m. A safety rope goes around the entire edge plus one rope across the width in the middle. How much rope is needed?
16 You're wrapping a ribbon around the edge of a rectangular gift box top that's 18 in × 12 in. You need an extra 8 inches for the bow. How much ribbon total?
17 A dog pen is shaped like a regular octagon with each side 5 ft. How much fencing is needed?
18 Challenge: A room is 15 ft × 12 ft. You want to add crown molding where the walls meet the ceiling. Crown molding comes in 8-foot pieces. How many pieces do you need to buy? (You can't use scraps shorter than 2 ft.)

🔑 Answer Key

1. 2(12)+2(8) = 40 ft | 2. 4(6.5) = 26 m | 3. 9+12+15 = 36 cm

4. 6(4) = 24 in | 5. 36 = 2(11) + 2w → 14 = 2w → w = 7 ft

6. P = 2(15)+2(10) = 50 ft, Cost = 50 × $4.50 = $225

7. 12×12 = 144 sq ft is the largest (square). Others: 16×8=128, 20×4=80, 14×10=140

8. Outside edges: 20+12+14+4+6+8 = 64 ft (trace the outside)

9. P = 2(16)+2(20) = 72 in = 6 ft, Cost = 6 × $2.25 = $13.50

10. P = 4(12) = 48 in = 4 ft, Cost = 4 × $2.25 = $9.00

11. 2(100)+2(60) = 320 m | 12. 1609 ÷ 320 ≈ 5.03, so about 5 laps

13. P = 2(14)+2(11) = 50 ft − 2(3) = 44 ft

14. P = 600 ft, Cost = 600 × $8.75 = $5,250

15. 2(25)+2(12)+12 = 86 m | 16. 2(18)+2(12)+8 = 68 in

17. 8(5) = 40 ft | 18. P = 54 ft, 54÷8 = 6.75, need 7 pieces
