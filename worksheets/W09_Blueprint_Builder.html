Worksheet 9: Blueprint Builder

Session 9 — Composite shapes and floor plans

Part 1: Decompose and Solve

1 An L-shaped room: main part 20 ft × 15 ft, smaller part 8 ft × 10 ft. Total area?
2 A T-shaped building: top bar 30 ft × 10 ft, vertical section 10 ft × 25 ft. Total area?
3 A rectangular yard 50 ft × 40 ft with a rectangular pool 15 ft × 10 ft removed. Grass area?

Part 2: Shapes with Semicircles

4 A window: rectangle 3 ft × 5 ft with a semicircle on top (diameter 3 ft). Total area?
5 A running track: rectangle 100 m × 60 m with semicircles on each short end (diameter 60 m). Total area inside the track?
6 An archway: rectangle 4 ft × 7 ft with a semicircle on top. Total area?

Part 3: 🏗️ Blueprint Challenge

You're helping a builder calculate flooring for different rooms. Tile costs $3.50/sq ft.
7 Master bedroom: 16 ft × 14 ft rectangle with a 6 ft × 4 ft closet alcove attached. Total tile area and cost?
8 Kitchen: 12 ft × 10 ft rectangle minus a 3 ft × 3 ft corner where the staircase goes. Tile area and cost?
9 Bathroom: 8 ft × 6 ft rectangle with a semicircular shower alcove (diameter 4 ft). Total tile area and cost?
10 Living room: 20 ft × 18 ft rectangle with a triangular bay window area (base 8 ft, height 4 ft). Total area and cost?
11 A hallway is 3 ft wide and shaped like an L. One section is 15 ft long and the other is 10 ft long. Total area?
Hint: Don't double-count the corner!
12 Challenge: The total budget for all flooring in problems 7-11 is $3,500. Will the builder stay under budget? Show the total cost.

🔑 Answer Key

1. 20×15 + 8×10 = 300 + 80 = 380 sq ft

2. 30×10 + 10×25 = 300 + 250 = 550 sq ft

3. 50×40 − 15×10 = 2000 − 150 = 1,850 sq ft

4. 3×5 + ½π(1.5²) = 15 + 3.53 = 18.53 sq ft

5. 100×60 + π(30²) = 6,000 + 2,826 = 8,826 sq m

6. 4×7 + ½π(2²) = 28 + 6.28 = 34.28 sq ft

7. 224 + 24 = 248 sq ft, $868 | 8. 120 − 9 = 111 sq ft, $388.50

9. 48 + ½π(4) = 48 + 6.28 = 54.28 sq ft, $189.98

10. 360 + ½(8)(4) = 360 + 16 = 376 sq ft, $1,316

11. 15×3 + 10×3 − 3×3 = 45 + 30 − 9 = 66 sq ft

12. $868 + $388.50 + $189.98 + $1,316 + $231 = $2,993.48 — Yes, under budget!
