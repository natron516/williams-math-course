You double ALL three dimensions of a box. The new volume is _____ times larger?
🤯 Volume Fact: When you double all three dimensions of a box, the volume multiplies by 2×2×2 = 8 times! A shipping box that's twice as big in every direction holds 8× as much stuff. This is why small increases in box size can mean huge jumps in shipping costs.
