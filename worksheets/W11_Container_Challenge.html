🤯 Cylinder Fun Fact: A standard soda can holds about 355 mL (355 cm³). Engineers carefully chose the radius and height to minimize metal used while maximizing volume. The "ideal" can that uses the least material for a given volume is actually almost a perfect cube shape — but round cans stack and stack better on shelves!
