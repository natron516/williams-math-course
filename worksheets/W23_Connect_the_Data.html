🔑 Answer Key

1. 1, 3, 5, 7, 11 | 2. 1, 4, 7, 10, 28 | 3. 4, 5, 6, 7, 9

4. y = 3x + 2 | 5. y = 2x + 3 | 6. y = 3x + 1

7. 0, 60, 120, 180, 240 | 8. 0, 45, 90, 135, 180

9. 180 − 135 = 45 miles | 10. 60t − 45t = 60 → 15t = 60 → t = 4 hours

11. 300/60 = 5 hours

15. h = 2w + 3 | 16. 2(6) + 3 = 15 cm | 17. 2w + 3 = 25, w = 11 weeks
