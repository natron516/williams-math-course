🔤 Math vs. Sheets — Side by Side

Every Google Sheets formula is really an equation. Cells are your variables!

✏️ Math Class
3x + 5
📊 Google Sheets
=3*A1 + 5
✏️ Math Class
Total = price × qty
📊 Google Sheets
=B2*C2
✏️ Math Class
Average = sum ÷ count
📊 Google Sheets
=SUM(B2:B5)/4
💡 The = sign in Sheets means "calculate this" — just like in algebra where = means "these sides are equal."
